More Information

Below details some of the more technical aspects of this model and gives tips on how to use these predictions.

Best Uses

  • Expecting these predictions to accurately reflect the fantasy output of these skaters is unrealistic
  • Best used for ranking or comparing players
  • Show strong rank ordering (best players at top of list and worst at bottom)
  • Tip: if a mediocre player shows up near the top of the rankings, they could be primed for an overperformance

Target and Features

  • Target: Daily fantasy points (assists = 8 points, goals = 12 points, powerplay points = 0.5, etc)
  • Features: Statistics from last game, 3, 10, and 14-day goals, assists, points moving average, position, home or away, opponent, opposing goalie

Training

  • This model used Robust Scaling with an xGBoost Regressor
  • xGBoost is a tree-structured regression technique that is very popular with tabular data
  • Months of experimenting with various features, other tree based techniques, and KNN and Monte Carlo simulations

Results

  • Minimized mean absolute error to narrow down model hyperparameters
  • Final model score hovered around 0.22
  • Great rank ordering but model is conservative, meaning it won't make out of the ordinary predictions, like a player scoring a hat trick
