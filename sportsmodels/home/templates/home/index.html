Our Mission

To many, "machine learning" is a black box, but it is consistently used by many odds makers and professional handicappers to generate predictions which guide their lines and decisions. We provide the average sports bettor and fantasy player with transparent tools and information.

Transparency

There is no such thing as a perfectly predictive model in sports. The randomness of sports makes it mathematically impossible to predict on a consistent basis. Gaining edge over odds makers comes from combining statistical tools with your own sports knowledge and intuition. For some people, these machine learning models will provide no value, but to others, when combined with their ability to analyze sports from a human perspective, can yield great results.

The Process

Our team applies machine learning models and methods to publically avaiable data to create predictive models for various leagues and sports. The models we use in production are the ones we feel the most confident in based on model testing and accuracy metrics. We will always be transparent about our methodologies and accuracy scores! They will always be displayed in clear language on each models homepage.

Models

As of now, we have a daily hockey model that can predict an individual skaters fantasy output for each game. Along with that, we develop individual mdoels for each golf tournament, predicting the finishing position of each golfer in the field. These models have taken months to develop, testing different methodologies and feature engineering.

Future

Let us know! We love exploring new model ideas. If there is data, we will try to model it! We are currently working on expanding our hockey models to predict all of the statistics for a skater (goals, assists, points) instead of solely their fantasy output. We also are currently developing our biggest model yet, a daily predictor for MLB hitters and pitchers.
